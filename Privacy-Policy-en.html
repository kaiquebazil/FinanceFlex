FinanceFlex Privacy Policy

English Version (United States)

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the FinanceFlex application ("we", "us", "our", "the Application") collects, uses, and protects your personal and financial information. By using FinanceFlex, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

FinanceFlex is designed to be a personal financial management application. The privacy and security of your data are our top priority.

  • Authentication Data (Firebase): If you choose to use the cloud synchronization feature, we will collect your email address and display name (optional) for authentication and data synchronization purposes through Firebase Authentication. Your password is securely stored and encrypted by Firebase, and we do not have direct access to it.
  • Financial Data: Your financial information, such as accounts, transactions, categories, credit cards, and budgets, is stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud synchronization, this data will be encrypted and stored in Firebase Firestore, associated with your user account. This data is used exclusively to provide the application's functionalities and is not accessed, analyzed, or shared by us for other purposes.
  • Usage and Diagnostic Data: We may collect anonymous information about how the application is used (e.g., features accessed, usage time, errors). This data is aggregated and used to improve the performance, stability, and user experience of the Application. We do not link this data to personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To operate and maintain the functionalities of FinanceFlex, including managing your finances and data synchronization.
  • To Improve the Application: To understand how users interact with the Application and identify areas for improvement, bug fixes, and new feature development.
  • Security: To protect the security and integrity of the Application and user data.
  • Communication (if applicable): To send important service-related notifications or updates, if you have opted to receive them.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personally identifiable information to third parties. Your information may only be shared in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google LLC) for authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase acts as our data processor and complies with applicable privacy laws. Your synchronized financial information is stored encrypted in Firebase Firestore.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes (e.g., a court order or subpoena).

4. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. If you delete your account, your authentication and financial data associated with cloud synchronization will be removed from Firebase. Data stored locally on your device is your responsibility and will be removed upon uninstalling the application or clearing application data.

5. Your Rights (GDPR/LGPD)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and LGPD, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data that we hold.
  • Rectification: Request the correction of incomplete or inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Data Portability: Request the transfer of your data to another service.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time, when processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the email provided in the "Contact Us" section.

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.
